1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided when you engage our services or create an account.
  • Support Information: Technical details and descriptions you provide when requesting troubleshooting or support services.
  • SMS-Related Information: Mobile phone number and SMS interaction records when you opt into our messaging services.
  • Usage Information: Software usage patterns and system data collected to improve service performance and reliability.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Website preferences and browsing data collected via cookies to enhance your online experience with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing IT services, customer support, and service notifications
  • Improving software functionality and service quality
  • Ensuring security of your systems and our infrastructure
  • Sending SMS updates, service alerts, and communications you have opted into

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., SMS gateways, cloud hosting providers), under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our clients or the public.
  • Business transfers such as mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, where your information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Restricted access controls to sensitive systems and data
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you promptly in the event of a breach affecting your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and correction: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and ask us to correct any inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Opt-out of SMS: Reply STOP to any SMS message we send to unsubscribe. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@tekvian.com.

6. SMS-Specific Privacy Practices

By opting into our SMS services, you agree to receive text messages from Tekvian Solutions, LLC. The following practices apply to our SMS communications:

  • Your mobile number is used solely for service-related communications and notifications.
  • No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS communications.
  • For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us at (520) 313-6002.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. We use two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly, including remembering your theme and language preferences.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve content and navigation.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be processed and stored in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and its processing in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
