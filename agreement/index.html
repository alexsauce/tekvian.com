This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is a legal and binding contract between TekVian Solutions, LLC (TekVian, We), an Arizona Limited Liability Company, and you (Customer, You), the Customer which is the signatory or acting on behalf of the legal entity that accepts this agreement.

A. Scope of Services

  1. This agreement governs all services that We perform or facilitate for You, as well as any licenses or products that TekVian sell or re-sell to Customer.
  2. The Services are not described in this Agreement; instead, We will provide You with a quote, proposal, service order or similar electronic document describing them (Services Proposal). By accepting the Services Proposal, You also agree to the terms of this Agreement. If You do not agree to the terms of this Agreement, then You should not accept the Services Proposal.

B. Responsibilities

TekVian agrees to:

  1. Services will be performed on a schedule, and in a prioritized manner, as We deem reasonable and necessary. Start dates may vary or deviate from the dates We state to You depending on the Service being provided.
  2. Services delivered by TekVian may include a mix of on-site, remote, and phone support.
  3. If We consider that a hardware or software issue requires vendor or OEM support, We may contact the vendor or OEM on your behalf and invoice You for all fees and costs involved in the process. We will obtain Customer authorization before incurring such expenses unless the circumstances require us otherwise.

Responsibilities of Customer:

Customer hereby agrees and is responsible for compensating Provider fees, costs, and expenses charged by TekVian for the services described in each Service Proposal. You are responsible for sales taxes and other taxes, or governmental fees associated with the services. You are also responsible for all freight, insurance, and related taxes.

  1. For on-site services of less than 1 hour, a minimum of one hour will be charged. For remote services a minimum of 15 minutes will be charged. Services will be charged by 15-minute increments after the first hour. Travel time for the consultant may be assessed at the consultant's normal hourly rate. Other travel costs for long-distance travel will be negotiated with Customer as needed and applicable.
  2. Customer will be invoiced and provided with service reports for all services provided. Payment terms for invoices are due on receipt. If payment is not received according to the terms of the agreement, TekVian reserves the right to suspend all services without further notice.
  3. TekVian reserves the right to modify payment terms and rates upon 30 days written notice to designated Customer representative.
  4. Fees that remain unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after the date on the applicable invoice will be subject to interest on the unpaid amounts at the lower of either 5% per month or the maximum allowable rate permitted by applicable law, until and including the date payment is received. We reserve the right to suspend part or all of the Services without prior notice in the event that payments are not timely received.
  5. Each term of each Service will renew automatically for additional terms equal in length to the initial term, unless Customer notifies TekVian in writing not less than thirty (30) days prior to the end of the initial term that it wishes to terminate such Service.

C. Access

You hereby grant to TekVian and our designated third-party vendors the right to monitor, diagnose, manipulate, communicate with, retrieve information from, and otherwise access any computer network, computer system, peripheral, or device acquired, installed, maintained, monitored, or operated by TekVian ("Environment") solely as necessary to enable us or our vendors to provide the Services.

If acceptance of an End User Agreement is required to provide Services to You, then You hereby grant TekVian permission to accept applicable agreements on your behalf. You agree to be bound by the terms of such End User Agreements.
  2. Portions of the Services may be acquired from, resold from, and/or rely upon the services of third-party vendors, manufacturers, or providers ("Third Party Provider"). Third Party Providers may provide services such as data hosting, help desk, malware detection, domain registration, and data backup/recovery ("Third Party Service"). You understand and agree that Third Party Providers are not our contractors or suppliers; We will not be responsible, and will be held harmless by You, for any failure of any Third Party Service or Third Party Provider.
  3. The Services may require TekVian to purchase certain "per seat" licenses from Microsoft ("NCE Licenses") to provide one or more of the following: Microsoft 365, Windows 365, Dynamics 365, and Microsoft Power Platform. As per Microsoft's requirements, NCE licenses cannot be canceled once purchased and cannot be transferred to any other customer. If We purchase NCE licenses on your behalf, those licenses may require a definite term of one (1) or three (3) years. You understand and agree that regardless of the reason for termination of the Services, You are required to pay for all applicable NCE Licenses in full for the entire term of those licenses. Provided You have paid in full, You will be permitted to use the applicable NCE Applications until expiration of their license terms, even if You terminate services with TekVian.
